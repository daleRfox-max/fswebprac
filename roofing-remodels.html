Helpful answers before you call

Do you handle roof replacement?

Yes. F&S handles roof replacement from removal and prep through installation, cleanup, and walkthrough.

Can one team handle siding and decking too?

Yes. Exterior improvements can often be sequenced together so materials, access, and cleanup are easier to manage.

Do you remodel kitchens and bathrooms?

Yes. F&S handles interior remodels, kitchens, bathrooms, additions, and whole-home updates.

What should I send for an estimate?

Photos, the property location, the age of the roof or space if known, timing, and what you want fixed or changed.
