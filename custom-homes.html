Common custom home questions

Can F&S help before plans are final?

Yes. Early conversations are useful for identifying site constraints, service needs, access, project sequence, and budget realities before construction starts.

Does F&S build garages and additions?

Yes. The team handles garages, additions, and structural improvements as standalone projects or as part of a larger home plan.

What areas do you serve?

F&S is based in Shelton and serves Mason County communities including Belfair, Hoodsport, Union, Allyn, and nearby counties.

How should I start?

Call or send project details, location, timing, photos, and any drawings you already have. F&S will help define the next step.
